# Toss-Impact-Analysis-in-T20-Cricket-IPL-2025
SQL + Power BI project analyzing toss impact in IPL 2025

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the impact of winning the toss on match outcomes in the Indian Premier League (IPL) 2025. The objective is to evaluate whether the toss provides a measurable advantage and how post-toss decisions influence match results.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Determine whether winning the toss increases match win probability  
- Analyze the impact of batting first vs fielding first  
- Evaluate how toss advantage varies across venues and teams  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Excel – Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- SQL – Data querying and aggregation  
- Power BI – Dashboard creation and visualization  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🔹 Overall Toss Impact
- Teams winning the toss won **59% of matches**, indicating a **moderate advantage**, but not a decisive factor  

### 🔹 Decision Impact (Bat vs Field)
- Batting first: **75% win rate**  
- Fielding first: **56% win rate**  

> ⚠️ Note: The batting-first sample size is relatively small (12 matches), which may affect reliability.

---

### 🔹 Venue-Based Insights
- Jaipur – **86% toss win conversion**  
- Chennai – **83% toss win conversion**  

👉 Suggests strong influence of venue conditions on match outcomes  

---

### 🔹 Team-Level Insights
- Delhi Capitals – **75% win rate after winning toss**  
- Punjab Kings – **73% win rate after winning toss**  

👉 Indicates effective utilization of toss advantage by certain teams  

---

## 🧠 Conclusion
Winning the toss provides a **noticeable but not decisive advantage** in T20 cricket. While strategic decisions such as batting first can influence outcomes, **team performance, execution, and venue conditions play a more significant role**.
